Our Boathouse

Canal Dock Boathouse, Inc. (CDBi) is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization in New Haven, Connecticut. CDBi is currently developing programs for the Canal Dock Boathouse, which it will lease from the City of New Haven, and will operate as a community-oriented, waterfront boating center. CDBi will eventually offer multiple types of recreational and competitive boating opportunities, from dragon boating, to rowing & sailing, as well as environmental education programs through a partnership with the University of New Haven.

Our Programs

CDBi is currently offering rowing memberships to experienced scullers who can demonstrate competence in a single shell. Before becoming cleared, all prospective members must first complete a USRowing Online Waiver (see below). In addition, the following forms must be submitted: CDBi Membership Application 2017; Declaration of Swimming Ability (age 25 & older); CDBi Swim Test Certification (under age 25); CDBi Waiver of Liability; CDBi Media Release Adult; or a CDBi Media Release Minor & Parent.
